Librebook - A randspoon product

Librebook is a free online social media made by therandomspoon.

The idea of Librebook was to give users a platform to communicate where any data is sold off to companies, because it just makes sense.

Why use Librebook instead of any other popular socialmedia? - Because Librebook believes in not selling off data and letting users speak freely

Isnt this kinda like facebook? "Look, a guy who builds a nice chair doesn's owe money to everyone who ever built a chair, okay? They came to me with an idea, I had a better one." - Mark Zuckerberg in the social network

In other words no. I understand the looks are pretty simular but the blue is completely different and so is the font also in terms of the name, Yes we took inspiration from facebook but not the company the object "facebook" was originally something like a "yearbook" - stackexchange. And we just put libre which is free in spanish infront because it sounded better than 'freebook'.
